About Me

Who is Sandip Walunj?

A dynamic personality bridging industry and academia

Sandip Walunj is the chairman of Seema Technologies, an IT Software Development & Training Company. He obtained his Diploma in Computer Technology from J.C.E.I.O.T College, Kuran, Pune (2000–2002), standing 64th in the MSBTE Board merit list. This achievement earned him admission to one of Pune's top colleges — Pune Institute of Computer Technology (P.I.C.T), graduating with First Class in B.E. Computer Engineering (2005).

He began his career in the IT industry as a Software Developer in .NET Technology for 2.1 years. During this period, he earned a Microsoft Certification in ASP.NET under C#.NET, becoming a Microsoft Certified Professional with a perfect score of 1000 out of 1000.

Driven by a keen interest in teaching, he transitioned to the education sector. He served as Lecturer at K.K. Wagh Polytechnic, Nashik (2 years), followed by 9+ years as Assistant Professor at Sandip Foundation's SITRC, Nashik. He completed his Masters in Computer Science and Engineering from M.I.T, Aurangabad, and published research in IEEE, Elsevier, and other prestigious journals.
